FudBud as a Revolutionary
Created as an initiative to bring ease into cooking for beginners



This website was founded by Teena Sebastian as an aid for people who strive for a healthier lifestyle This website aims in helping people by allowing them to choose their meals based on the ingredients they already posses This in turn reduces the time spent searching for a recipe, only to miss out on the meal due to missing ingredients This app encourages users to try out new recipes from verified sources as the knowledge that we posses all the ingredients to make the recipe may encourage them in that way
